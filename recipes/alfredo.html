Alfredo Recipe

Cheesey Alfredo

Made with simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • Penne Noodles
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Penne Noodles
  2. Cook Alfredo sauce at the same time
  3. Mix penne noodles, alfredo sauce, and olive oil
  4. Top with Parmesean Cheese
