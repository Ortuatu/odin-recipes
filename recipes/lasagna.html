Cheesey Lasagna

Made with simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Lay noodle base
  2. Cover with tomato sauce, ground beef, and cheese
  3. Repeat to desired layer amount
  4. Top with noodles and cheese
  5. Bake until golden brown
